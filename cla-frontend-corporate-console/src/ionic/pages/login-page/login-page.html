Authorize Your Coperate Developers

Ensure that your corporate developers are able to contribute code productively while streamling how CLAs get signed by your company:

  • Whitelist your developers by domain, Gihub handle, and email address.
  • Capture e-signature with DocuSign

How to Start Using EasyCLA as a Corporate Contributor

Step 1
Set up your company on EasyCLA
  • The Project Manager, often the project maintainer, will have added uploaded the appropraite CLA
  • When one of your developers attemps to contribute to the projec, they will send a request for the CLA to be signed and that they ( and other developers ) to be authorized to contribute.
  • Create your account to manage and sign the CLA, as well as whitelist developers from this page.
Step 2
Sign the CLA
  • Either submit the address of your authorized signatory or sign the CLA directly yourself.
  • Signatures are captured electronically using DocuSign.
Step 3
Whitelist Your Developers

Once you have signed the CLA for the assocaited project, you can whitelist developers in several ways:

  • By Domain
  • By Guthub handle
  • By email address
